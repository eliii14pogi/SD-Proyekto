SAFETY GUIDELINES

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

  • All students and faculty must wear appropriate PPE when working in the laboratory.
  • Lab coats must be worn at all times.
  • Safety goggles are required for all experiments.
  • Heat-resistant gloves must be used when handling hot materials.
  • Chemical-resistant gloves are required when handling chemicals.

Chemical Handling

  • All chemicals must be clearly labeled.
  • Never return unused chemicals to their original containers.
  • Always read the Safety Data Sheet (SDS) before using any chemical.
  • Report all chemical spills immediately to laboratory staff.

Emergency Procedures

  • Know the location of fire extinguishers, eye wash stations, and emergency showers.
  • Emergency exit routes are posted at each door.
  • Emergency contact: Muntinlupa Emergency Hotline at 137-175.
  • For all serious injuries, call emergency services at 911.
